Welcome

Dear colleagues,

On Monday, 17th of June 2024, the 24th Day of Sociology will take place in Nijmegen. This year, the event is organized by the Department of Sociology at Radboud University in collaboration with the Dutch Sociological Association (NSV) and the Flemish Association for Sociology (VVS).

Theme

Climate Change - Where is the Sociological Research?

Climate change is also a social issue. However, sociologists often seem absent within research consortia investigating climate change.

  • Is this a concern?
  • What could sociological research contribute?
  • How can we initiate sociological research on the social issue of climate change?
  • Are (many) sociologists hesitant about participatory action research?

Research into the causes and adverse effects of climate change requires a systemic approach. But what does that mean? We hope that the keynote speakers will inspire us. They will share their experiences within transdisciplinary research consortia.

Call for Papers

Dear colleagues,

On Monday, June 17, 2024, the 24th Sociology Day takes place in Nijmegen. This year, the conference will be organized by the Sociology department of Radboud University, in collaboration with the Dutch Sociological Association (NSV) and the Flemish Association for Sociology (VVS).

As of today, paper abstracts (max. 250 words) can be submitted via the NSV website (click here for a direct link to the submission form). If possible, provide a concise description of the research questions, theoretical framework, methods and (preliminary) findings. When submitting, please indicate for which panel you want to apply. In addition to general panels, there are also various thematic panels, some of which are still open for abstracts. Papers that do not immediately fit within a theme can be submitted for the â€™open panel. On the registration form you can indicate whether you want to present in Dutch and/or English. The organization strives for a good distribution of Dutch and English sessions. Authors can present a maximum of 1 paper themselves, but can be co-authors on multiple papers in the program.

Deadline for abstract submission is March 22, 2024. After the closing date, the abstracts will be assessed and assigned to a panel by the organizing committee. If your paper does not fit into the desired panel, we will try to place you in another suitable panel.

You will be informed in mid-April 2024 whether your abstract has been accepted and to which panel you have been assigned. All participants (speakers, panel chairmen, other interested parties) must then register via the NSV website to participate in the Sociology Day (this is possible from mid-April). Participation is not possible without registration and payment in advance.

Information on the Sociology day can be found at www.dvds2024.nl. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us at dvds2024@nsv-sociologie.nl.

Please feel free to distribute this call to colleagues and interested parties. We are looking forward to your participation!

Best wishes, the organizing committee,

Nella Geurts, Bas Hofstra, Klara Raiber, Jochem Tolsma and Ellen Verbakel
