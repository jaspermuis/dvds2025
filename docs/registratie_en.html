Registration

Registration form

You can register for the Sociology Day (DvdS), to be held on Monday June 17 2024, via this QR code or this link. You will be redirected to a form and payment system. Deadline for registration is 1st of June.

Participation fee

Members of the Dutch Sociological Association (NSV) receive a discount on participation in the Sociology Day. If you are not yet a member, but would like to become a member, you can indicate this on the registration form.
Non-student Bachelor or Master student
Participation in DvdS for NSV members 100euro 25euro
Participation in DvdS + NSV membership (until 31-12-2025) 166euro 50euro
Participation in DvdS without NSV membership 175euro 25euro
Participation in DvdS for NSV honorary members free

Participation fees are non-refundable.
